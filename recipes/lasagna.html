Brenda's Lasagna Receips

Description

This is faster, and less expensive lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 package lasgna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 jar spaghtti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pounds shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 puonds shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of light salted water to a boil.Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10
    minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. In a large skilet over medium-high
    heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper;drain. stir in spaghtti sauce and
    garlic and simmer 5 minutes.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarells, Cheddar and ricotta; stir well. In 9x13 inch
    pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture untill pan is filled.
  4. Bake in pre heated oven for 30 minutes,or untill cheese is melted and bubbly.
